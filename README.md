# Shore-Squad
RPG/RTS game

A recent discovery troubled a world where magic is tabooed and those whose magic courses through their veins persecuted.
A stone, capable of being imbued with magical properties for anyone to us, has been discovered on a previously unknown territory to the east.
To escape your dull life, to regain your freedom or else; embark upon a journey to discover this world full of dancer, obstacles but also riches.

The player controls a party of up to 6 characters in order to survives in the new world.
The player is subject to environmental dangers such as; cold, heat, starvation, creatures, but most importantly time itself.
Enemy faction also try to step foot and hold position on this new territory in order to extract the stone either to use it for their power, sell it or destroy it..
